The Story Behind the Work

Hi, I'm Ann.

For over a decade, I climbed the corporate ladder in marketing, checking every box of success — the title, the salary, the busy calendar. On paper, I had it all. On the inside, I was running on fumes.

After a health scare forced me to slow down, I realized I had been living a life designed by other people's expectations. That journey of rebuilding my own life led me to become a Certified Professional Co-Active Coach (CPCC) and an Associate Certified Coach (ACC) with the International Coaching Federation.

I don't believe in "fixing" you because you aren't broken. You already possess the wisdom and strength to create a fulfilling life — my job is simply to be the strategic partner and compassionate mirror who helps you clear away the fog.

FAQ

Your Questions, Answered

How much does coaching cost? +
Pricing varies based on package length and frequency. I offer 3-month and 6-month containers. Instead of a price list, I invite you to a free discovery call where we can discuss the right investment level for the transformation you're seeking.
Is coaching like therapy? +
No. Coaching is forward-focused and action-oriented. We work on where you are now and where you want to go, designing strategies for the future. It is not a substitute for mental health treatment or diagnosing past trauma.
How do we meet? +
All sessions are held via secure Zoom video. This allows me to work with clients across the country — and for you to attend in your comfiest clothes.
How long is the commitment? +
Sustainable change takes time. I ask for a minimum commitment of 3 months. This gives us enough runway to break old patterns and solidify new, healthier habits.
I'm not sure exactly what I need yet. Is that okay? +
Absolutely. That's what Step 1 is for. Most clients come to me with a feeling of "stuckness" rather than a neatly packaged problem. We figure it out together.
